# Jigocoin Roadmap

## Mission

Develop and maintain an independent, decentralized and sustainable blockchain network guided by the principles of Seiryoku Zenyo and Jita Kyoei.

## Current State

* Independent blockchain network
* Active node infrastructure
* Independent monetary policy
* Proof of Work consensus
* Community-operated mining

## Near-Term

### Infrastructure

* Strengthen node monitoring and alerting.
* Improve consensus monitoring across network nodes.
* Increase redundancy of seed and service nodes.

### Documentation

* Maintain accurate technical documentation.
* Expand operational and deployment guides.
* Document network topology and infrastructure.

### Network Growth

* Encourage independent node operators.
* Improve network resilience and decentralization.
* Support sustainable mining participation.

## Mid-Term

### Ecosystem

* Improve block explorer capabilities.
* Enhance wallet usability and operational tooling.
* Improve observability and operational metrics.

### Governance

* Maintain transparent decision-making processes.
* Document consensus-critical changes and network upgrades.

## Long-Term

* Preserve network independence.
* Maintain a sustainable monetary system.
* Promote decentralization and long-term resilience.
* Continue development guided by Seiryoku Zenyo and Jita Kyoei.
