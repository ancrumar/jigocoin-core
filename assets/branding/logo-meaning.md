# Meaning of the Jigocoin Logo

The Jigocoin logo is inspired by the history, philosophy and symbolism of Judo, the martial art founded by Professor Jigoro Kano.

More than a visual identifier, the logo represents the values, principles and traditions that inspire the Jigocoin project.

## The Letter J

The central element of the logo is the letter **J**.

It simultaneously represents:

* **Jigocoin**
* **Judo**
* **Professor Jigoro Kano (1860–1938)**, founder of Judo and one of the most influential educators and thinkers of modern Japan.

The letter serves as a visual bridge between the project, the art of Judo and the enduring legacy of Professor Kano, whose teachings continue to inspire generations around the world.

## The Black Color

The J is displayed in black, representing the **Kuro Obi** (black belt).

In Judo, the black belt symbolizes the transition from the learning stages (**Kyū**) to the stages of mastery (**Dan**).

For Jigocoin, the black belt represents discipline, perseverance, continuous improvement and the pursuit of excellence through study, practice and experience.

## The Inclined Position

The J is intentionally inclined slightly to the left (**hidari**).

This inclination symbolizes the traditional standing bow of Judo (**Ritsu Rei**), an expression of respect, humility and mutual recognition between practitioners.

Respect is one of the fundamental values of Judo and remains a guiding principle for the Jigocoin community.

## The Two Bars

The two horizontal bars crossing the center of the J symbolize the **knot of the black belt**.

In Judo, the belt knot unites and secures the belt around the practitioner. In the Jigocoin logo, it represents the point where philosophy and action come together.

The two bars also symbolize the two fundamental principles taught by **Professor Jigoro Kano**:

* **Seiryoku Zenyo (精力善用)** — Maximum Efficient Use of Energy
* **Jita Kyoei (自他共栄)** — Mutual Welfare and Benefit

These principles form the philosophical foundation of Jigocoin and guide its technical, economic and community development.

## The Tatami

The background of the logo is inspired by the traditional colors of Judo competition tatami.

### Green Area

The green central area represents the combat and practice zone:

* **Randori** (free practice)
* **Shiai** (competition)

It symbolizes action, learning, experimentation and continuous development.

### Red Boundary

The surrounding red ring represents the danger zone traditionally used in competition tatami.

It reminds practitioners of boundaries, awareness and responsibility.

Within the context of Jigocoin, it symbolizes risk management, operational discipline and the need to maintain balance within a decentralized network.

The green and red colors intentionally reflect the appearance of traditional competition tatami used for decades in Judo tournaments around the world.

## A Symbol of Balance

Taken as a whole, the logo represents the union of:

* Tradition and innovation.
* Individual responsibility and collective benefit.
* Technical efficiency and ethical conduct.
* Competition and cooperation.
* Mastery through continuous improvement.

These concepts reflect the vision of Jigocoin as a decentralized monetary network inspired by the enduring principles of Judo.

## Acknowledgement

The Jigocoin project respectfully acknowledges the intellectual, educational and ethical legacy of **Professor Jigoro Kano**.

His teachings transcend sport and continue to provide inspiration for personal development, responsible leadership and mutual prosperity.

The values embodied in Judo remain a lasting source of guidance for the philosophy and long-term vision of Jigocoin.
